Why this tool exists
Grounded in Indigenous ways of knowing
Two-Eyed Seeing
Indigenous healing and Western approaches held equally — neither replaces the other.
Medicine Wheel
Wellness across mental, emotional, physical, and spiritual dimensions.
2Spirit-affirming
2Spirit identity is a strength. Dedicated 2Spirit crisis support listed throughout.
Non-police first
TCCS Indigenous mobile crisis — led by 2-Spirited People of the 1st Nations — offered as the first emergency option.
Cultural continuity
Connection to culture, Elders, and land are among the strongest protective factors for Indigenous peoples.
Nothing stored
Every answer stays on your own device. No accounts, no servers, no tracking.
